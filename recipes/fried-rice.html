Fried Rice

Description

Simple, authentic, 10-minute fried rice

Ingredients

  • Day-old rice (refrigerated)
  • 3 eggs
  • Garlic
  • Shallot
  • Spring onions
  • Neutral Oil
  • MSG

Steps

  1. Prep your ingredients: Chop green onions. If using other vegetables, cut them into small pieces.
  2. Scramble eggs: In a separate pan, scramble eggs and set aside.
  3. Heat oil: Heat oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  4. Add rice: Stir-fry the cold rice in the hot oil until it becomes slightly crispy and golden brown.
  5. Mix-ins: Add scrambled eggs, green onions, and any other desired vegetables to the rice. Stir-fry until everything is heated through.
  6. Season: Pour soy sauce over the mixture and stir to combine. Taste and adjust seasonings as needed.

Enjoy your homecooked fried rice!
