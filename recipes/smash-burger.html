Smash Burger

Description

A nice, juicy, cheesy smash burger complete with that crispy lace

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Hamburger buns
  • Cheese slices (american cheese)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Butter or oil

Steps

  1. Prep the patties: Divide ground beef into equal-sized balls.
  2. Heat the pan: Heat a cast iron skillet or heavy-bottomed pan over high heat.
  3. Smash the burgers: Place beef balls on the hot pan and immediately press down with a spatula to form a thin patty. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Cook the burgers: Cook for about 1-2 minutes per side, or until desired doneness. Top with cheese in the last minute of cooking.
  5. Assemble the burger: Toast the buns, if desired. Place cooked burger on bottom bun and add your favorite toppings.

Enjoy your homecooked smash burger!
