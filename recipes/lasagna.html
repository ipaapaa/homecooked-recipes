Lasagna

Description

A nice authentic Italian lasagna that is sure to fill up your cravings.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Bechamel Sauce
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 2 whole onions
  • Celery
  • Carrots
  • MSG
  • Ground beef

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles: Boil lasagna noodles according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Brown the beef: Cook ground beef in a pan until browned. Drain any excess grease.
  3. Combine sauce and beef: Stir marinara sauce into the cooked ground beef.
  4. Layer lasagna: Spread a thin layer of sauce in a baking dish. Layer with cooked noodles, ricotta cheese, and a sprinkle of Parmesan. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of sauce and mozzarella cheese.
  5. Bake: Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30 minutes. Uncover and bake for an additional 10 minutes until bubbly and golden brown.

Enjoy your homemade lasagna!
